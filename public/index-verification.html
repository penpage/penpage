What is PenPage?

PenPage is a privacy-first note-taking web application designed for users who value speed, simplicity, and data ownership. Whether you're capturing quick thoughts, writing documentation, or organizing your ideas, PenPage provides a distraction-free environment with powerful features.

PenPage stores your notes locally in your browser by default, ensuring your data stays on your device. Export or backup anytime — your data is never locked in.
