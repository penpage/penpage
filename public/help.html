Getting Started

PenPage is a privacy-first note-taking application that stores your notes locally in your browser. No account required, no data sent to servers.

  1. Create a Page - Click the + button to create a new note
  2. Start Writing - Use Markdown shortcuts or just type naturally
  3. Organize - Create folders and workspaces to organize your notes
  4. Backup - Go to Settings > Save to export your notes
  5. Export - Go to Settings > Save to backup or export as Markdown

Frequently Asked Questions

Privacy & Security

  • Does PenPage access my note content? - No. PenPage runs entirely in your browser. Your notes are stored in IndexedDB on your device and are never sent to our servers.
  • Where is my data stored? - All data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. Your data never leaves your device.

Data & Backup

  • Will clearing browser data delete my notes? - Yes. That's why we strongly recommend regular backups (Settings > Save).
  • Can I use PenPage on multiple devices? - Yes! Export/import backups to move notes between devices.

Features

  • What Markdown syntax is supported? - PenPage supports headings (#), bold (**), italic (*), lists, code blocks, links, images, and more.
  • Does the PWA work offline? - Yes! Once installed, PenPage works fully offline.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl/Cmd + B - Bold
  • Ctrl/Cmd + I - Italic
  • Ctrl/Cmd + U - Underline
  • Ctrl/Cmd + K - Add link
  • Ctrl/Cmd + Z - Undo
  • Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Z - Redo
